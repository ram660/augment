# Market Landscape

## Three Competitive Categories

### Category 1: AI Design Tools ($50B TAM)

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness | How We Win |
|------------|----------|----------|------------|
| **HomeDesigns.ai** | 50+ styles, viral marketing | No cost estimation, no execution | We add cost intelligence + contractor matching |
| **Planner5D** | Comprehensive 3D tools | Manual process, steep learning curve | We automate with AI (2 min vs. hours) |
| **Reimagine Home AI** | AR visualization | Slow (15 min), mobile-only | We're faster (2 min) and more accurate |

**Key Insight:** Design tools show "what's possible" but don't help execute. We bridge the gap.

---

### Category 2: Contractor Platforms ($180B TAM)

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness | How We Win |
|------------|----------|----------|------------|
| **Houzz Pro** | Large network, established brand | No AI, expensive ($65-165/mo), complex | We're AI-first, cheaper, simpler |
| **Thumbtack/Angi** | Large contractor network | Unqualified leads, no visualization | We send pre-qualified leads with RFPs |
| **HomeStars** | Strong in Canada, detailed reviews | Canada-only, no design tools | We're global + add visualization |

**Key Insight:** Contractor platforms connect people but don't help visualize or budget. We add intelligence.

---

### Category 3: Field Service Software ($20B TAM)

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness | How We Win |
|------------|----------|----------|------------|
| **Jobber** | Job tracking, CRM, invoicing | Contractor-only, no lead gen | We're complementary (send them leads) |
| **ServiceTitan** | Enterprise-grade, comprehensive | Very expensive ($1,000+/mo), enterprise-only | We serve different market + send leads |

**Key Insight:** Field service software manages existing jobs. We generate NEW qualified leads.  
**These are integration partners, not competitors.**
