## üí° Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Take Good Photos
- Use natural light when possible
- Take photos straight-on (not at an angle)
- Include the whole room or area you want to change
- Higher resolution = better results

### Tip 2: Generate Multiple Variations
- Always generate 3-4 variations
- AI gives you different options to choose from
- You might love one you didn't expect!

### Tip 3: Make One Change at a Time
- Transform paint first, save your favorite
- Then transform flooring using that result
- Build up your complete vision step by step
- This gives you the most control

### Tip 4: Use Product Suggestions
- Enable "Suggest Products" when available
- Get real products from Canadian stores
- See actual prices and availability
- Click through to buy immediately

### Tip 5: Save Everything
- Create a free account to save your transformations
- Build a project with all your ideas
- Share with family, friends, or contractors
- Come back anytime to review

### Tip 6: Try Freeform Prompts for Complex Changes
- If you want to change multiple things, use freeform prompts
- Be specific: "white shaker cabinets, quartz counters, subway tile backsplash"
- AI understands natural language
- You'll get product suggestions for everything
